The Journey In EPC

Being an executive member in the open source club of the college added totally new experiences to my life journey. Being a representative of one of the leading club of the college helped be add various attributes to my personality that helped me grow into a better individual, well I say a better technical individual.

My interview for the executive

I believe in thinking out of the box. Academics alone is too mainstream, I wanted to explore engineering beyound that. This spirit led me to go for being the executive member of the club.

Writing for the EPC magazine

My fetish for writing knew its extremitites and extended its boundaries when i got acquainted to the technical club magazine. It enhanced my perspective towards technology and helped me understand the innovations better.

The Only Girl

I was long acquainted known to the fact that my stream was mostly conquered by men. Being the executive of the club made me live that fact. Attending meetings as the only girl, working in a group as the only girl added to my confidence and the love for engineering grew stronger.

The awaited Magazine Launch

One of the main event of our tenure, The EPC magazine Launch was one of the extraordinary experince I went across in life. When you dream anything and work hard to make that dream come true together in a group, the satisfaction we achieve knows no boundaries after that.

Added Responsibility

The journey of personal discoveries was now accompanied with the responsibility to direct the new comers to pursue great steps of hardwork in order to live a life that is least chosen my many people. This was one new experience that changed my view towards life.

Transforming into a totally new individual

The immaturity I had when I joined the club was now slowly fading away. The bunch of experiences tranformed my perspective towards life in so many ways. I will always remain indebted to the club for making me a better person than what I was.
